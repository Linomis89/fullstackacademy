Welcome to The Wild Animal Mini-Zoo internship program.

We here, at The Wild Animal Mini-zoo, are committed to promoting animal welfare, conservation, and education. Our team is focused on establishing a safe and immersive environment for our animals while engaging our community through various educational programs and interactive experiences. We encourage and nurture the passion and love for nature and wildlife.
